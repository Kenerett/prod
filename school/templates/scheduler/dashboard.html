Welcome, {{ user.get_full_name }}!

You are now in the Schedule Management System designed for students and teachers. Here you can fully manage the process of creating, editing, and exporting schedules. The system was built to make your work easier and eliminate the need to manually create schedules on paper or in separate files.

What you can do here:

• Create schedules — add groups, teachers, courses, classrooms, and class times. The system organizes everything and generates a clear schedule automatically.

• Edit schedules — make changes at any time: reschedule classes, change teachers or rooms, add new subjects or remove unnecessary ones.

• Export schedules — save the final schedule in Excel or PDF format to easily share it with teachers and students. Perfect for printing or emailing.

Why is this useful?

With this system, you save time and reduce the chance of mistakes. No more manual conflict checking or overlapping schedules — the system does it automatically. Teachers always have updated information about their classes, and students can quickly access the latest schedule for the week.

The system is also flexible: it can be used for different faculties, groups, and courses. You can manage multiple schedules, keep them up to date, and even store a history of changes.

Tips for using the system:

• Always double-check the schedule before saving. • Update schedules regularly so teachers and students always see accurate information. • Use the export function to archive schedules in convenient formats. • If necessary, create separate schedules for different groups or courses.
