Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about the STMS

Yes, a teacher can change grades an unlimited number of times, but only until the established deadline.

For example: if the Final exam starts on May 10th and the semester ends on May 25th, the teacher can make any changes to student grades during the period from May 10th to May 25th.

After the specified date, it will be impossible to change grades.

👉 In the electronic journal, students can:

  • view their grades;
  • track absences and attendance;
  • access educational materials uploaded by teachers;
  • monitor overall progress in subjects.

👉 Teachers are required to:

  • enter grades;
  • record attendance and absences;
  • upload educational materials for students.

👉 In the near future, the following is planned:

  • adding notifications about new grades and materials;
  • expanding analytics for students and teachers;
  • simplifying feedback through the Support system.

👉 Support is the system's helpdesk. There you can:

  • for students — report incorrect grades, attendance errors, or issues with uploaded materials;
  • for teachers — contact regarding problems with entering grades, uploading materials, and other technical difficulties;
  • for all users — share ideas for system development, comments, and suggestions.

👉 The system is only accessible from within the territory of Azerbaijan.

👉 The electronic journal can be used on any device:

  • computer,
  • laptop,
  • tablet,
  • smartphone.
